Website Evaluations

Website 1: Viz

Viz media is an american entertainment company that focuses on the publishing and distribusion of manga, as well as the licensing of japanese film and anime.

  • Contrast - Strong use of contrast with eye catching visuals and easy to distinguish content.
  • Repetition - Consistent design elements such as the font, colors, and general page layout.
  • Alignment - Well organized layout with aligned elements, making it easy to navigate and scan the content.
  • Proximity - Effective grouping and use of space.

Website 2: Gaiaonline

Gaiaonline is an anime-themed social networking and forums based website with its own economy that allows users to create avatars and interact with one another through games, roleplaying, and forum discussion.

  • Contrast - Good use of contrast with bolder colors for the important sections such as the navigation bars. But it is dense and can make things less noticable
  • Repetition - Consistent design elements such as the font, colors, and navigation menu.
  • Alignment - The site layout is cluttered and lacks clean alignment.
  • Proximity - Elements are grouped well together but the amount of information leads to confusion within the space.
