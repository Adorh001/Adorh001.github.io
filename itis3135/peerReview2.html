Website 1: Yilin's Graphic Design Gallery

This website is simple, shows off the art of the subject quite well, and is well organized. The design is Consistent, has good font and colors and alignment. Overall this is a good page for an art showcase with the only critisism being the color palette being a bit lacking.

  • Contrast - The image has vibrant colors that draw attention and complements the gallery concept. The call-to-action text in the gallery section uses a bold purple font, which helps differentiate it from the rest of the page.
  • Repetition - The consistent purple theme across the page creates a unified brand identity. Repetition in the font style (e.g., the use of clean sans-serif fonts) maintains cohesion.
  • Alignment - Central alignment of elements like the title, gallery title, and image creates an aesthetically balanced layout.
  • Proximity - The grouping of the gallery title and description near the image conveys a clear relationship between text and visuals.
