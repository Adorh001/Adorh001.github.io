Website Evaluations

Website 1: AJ Tutoring

AJ Tutoring is a Tutoring site where you are able to set up a Tutoring session with Anusha Joshi for reasonable pricing and with decent testimonials.

  • Contrast - The text contrasts well with the background, thanks to the dark text on a light background. However, the navigation bar lacks significant visual distinction. The red horizontal lines blend into the blue background, reducing their effectiveness. A more distinct color scheme could enhance the separation between elements.
  • Repetition - Repetition is evident in the typography across headers and body text, maintaining consistency. However, the design could benefit from more repeated stylistic elements to unify the visual identity.
  • Alignment - The alignment is clean and centered, giving the page a professional and structured look.
  • Proximity - The spacing between the header, navigation bar, and body content is adequate.
