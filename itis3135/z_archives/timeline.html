History of our collection

The history of our games:

  • Megas XLR: Megas vs the Universe - Created by "This is Pop" to promote the TV series Megas XLR. This 2D shooter highlights the show's destructive nature.
  • Bullet Fighting Time - Released in 2004, this arcade fighter mimics The Matrix. Players dodge bullets and combat enemies with kicks and punches.
  • Samurai Jack: Code of Samurai - A 2D rogue-like arcade game promoting Samurai Jack. Use honor and valor to fight Aku.
  • Super Smash Flash 1 - A fan-made Super Smash Brothers game with 28 playable characters, including fan creations.
